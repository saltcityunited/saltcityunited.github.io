The Riot was established to unify the most die-hard supporters of Real Salt Lake (RSL), bringing together passionate fan groups under one banner. Since its creation, The Riot has evolved into a crucial part of RSL’s identity, with its members playing a key role in energizing the team and intimidating opponents. Known for non-stop chanting, drumming, and waving flags, The Riot transforms every match into a spectacle of support. Over the years, they have built a fierce reputation for their unwavering loyalty and dedication.

Each group within The Riot—including Salt City United, Riot Brigade, Rogue Cavaliers Brigade, and La Barra Real—contributes its own unique energy. Together, they form a unified, powerful force that drives Real Salt Lake forward with every match. The Riot’s commitment goes beyond just supporting the team; it creates an atmosphere that makes the stadium a fortress for RSL and a challenge for every opponent.

If you're ready to be part of the energy, passion, and relentless support, join The Riot and help make every matchday an unforgettable experience.
